Features

Everything Night Shift forgot

Gloam talks straight to your display's gamma tables, so the warmth goes far deeper — and every adjustment is yours.

500 K to 6500 K

Slide from neutral daylight down past candlelight to a pure, blue-free red — for late nights, dark rooms, and tired eyes.

One-tap presets

Reading, Candle, Night, and Pure Red are a click away. Tune temperature, intensity, and dimming independently.

Sunset to sunrise

Let Gloam follow the sun from your location, or set fixed on/off times. It re-asserts itself after sleep and wake.

Global hotkey

Toggle the filter from anywhere with ⌥⌘R — remap it to whatever you like. No need to reach for the menu bar.

Per-display calibration

Multiple monitors behave differently. Dial in a strength factor for each one so they all land at the same warmth.

Pause & smooth fades

Pause for 15, 30, or 60 minutes when a color-true task pops up. Every transition eases in gently — no jarring flash.
